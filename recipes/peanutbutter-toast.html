Peanut Butter Toast

Description

A crispy duo of peanutbutter on sliced toast

You will first need these ingredients and supplies

  • A toaster
  • Peanut butter
  • A spreading utensil (butter knife ftw!)
  • Your favorite bread, sliced!

To replicate that yummy image above, follow these simple af steps!

  1. Plug in your toaster
  2. Place your toast into the toaster, and turn it on
  3. Prepare your peanutbutter and knife while bread is toasting
  4. When bread is finished, lathera a butt load of peanutbutter onto your toasted bread
  5. Profit!
