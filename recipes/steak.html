Medium Rare Steak!

Description

A gorgeous side profile of a medium rare steak fried to perfection

You will first need these ingredients and supplies

  • Olive Oil or Lard
  • A cast iron or non stick frying pan
  • A flipping utensil of your choice (tongs, stainless steel flipper etc)
  • Seasonings of your choice (I use salt and pepper exclusively)
  • And most important, a dethawed slab of beef from the fridge

To reproduce this recipe, simply follow these steps

  1. Dry out the steak with a paper towel
  2. Preheat the oven element to medium high
  3. Drizzle a tablespoon of olive oil or lard onto the pan
  4. Apply your choice amount of seasonings to both sides of steak, and massage in
  5. When pan sizzles a drop of water, lay down slab of steak. Should be sizzling!
  6. Let the steak fry for 3-4 minutes
  7. Flip the steak
  8. Let sizzle for another 3-4 minutes
  9. Both sides now should now be dark but not charred, all exteriors should show no pink, and after slicing through, there should be a healthy dose of pink meat
  10. Enjoy!
